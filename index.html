About Clare Central Motel

Maggie, Harry and Sophie welcome you to experience all that the Clare Valley has to offer in their 30 room accommodation motel, restaurant and bar. Clare Central Motel contains various room types to accommodate your needs including self contained family rooms, king kitchenette, spa rooms, twin rooms and disabled rooms.

Continental or cooked breakfast is available daily and delivered to your room. In Winter, indulge in local wine and a cheese plate by our cosy fire, or relax and soak up the sun by our salt water pool with lawn area in summer.

Cook your own BBQ with local meats or eat at our Burger Bar @ Central.

Clare Central Motel is close to local wineries and walking distance to the centre of town.

At Your Service

Functions and catering

Why not have your next celebration with us? Clare Central has a function/conference room available for hire and catering is also available. We can cater for anniversaries, high tea, birthday parties, kids parties, business meetings and just about anything.

Room and facilities

Microwave, kettle, fridge with tea, coffee, milk and biscuits restocked with daily room service. Car parking is available outside every room with a large block next door for trucks, vans and caravans. Cooked or continental breakfast available on request. Cook your own BBQ at the communal BBQ area with local meats or dine in our restaurant, Burger Bar @ Central. Salt water swimming pool.

Additional services

Landry service, packed lunches and catering for functions.
